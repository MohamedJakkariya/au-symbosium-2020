PRESENT IT

Start Date : 22-02-2020
Deadline Date : 04-03-2020
Notification : 06-03.2020
Each presenter will get 20 min for presentation + 10 min for Q & A. The presentation should be only in MS PowerPoint (ppt /pptx) or PDF formats. The first slide should display the title of the presentation & name of the presenter. Presentation time restrictions will be strictly followed Presenters are requested to send your presentation in advance to avoid any last-minute technology issues. The deadline for presentation submission: The Paper Presentation Event aims to provide the best platform to showcase your research ideas in front of an esteemed panel of judges. If you have carried out research works in the computer-science fields, we would be more than happy to hear your ideas out! Prepare a presentation and present it to grab exciting prizes!

POSTARIZE

In this event participants should prepare the poster from the given topic related to Computer Science maximum content in the poster should be pictorially represented.

CODE COLLESIUM

In this event a written test is conducted with set of question related to the c-language and certain time limit is given and the participants has to complete the test within the time limit. Depending upon their performance they are selected for the further rounds.

DARE TO CODE

This event contains 3 rounds.The round 1 will be a written test containing 20 questions related to debugging.The shortlisted participants will enter into second round.In second round the participants will be given erroneous code.points awarded on the basis of errors detected and corrected.The shortlisted participants of second round will enter into third round.The third round will be blind coding.Time limit is 20 mins for all rounds.

GADGET REVIEW

In this event a written test is conducted with a set of questions related to the software or hardware, before that we allot a particular amount of time to search about the software or hardware. Then the written test is conducted with the time limit. Depending upon their performance participants are selected for final round

BURST OUT

We present you with an all Techinical-quiz that covers wide range of topics pertaining to the subjects under our own department CSE. Supposedly fun and challenging, this quiz should throughly encourage you to test your techncal knowledge and put your brain to work.

QUERY FETCH

In this event, the participants will be asked 20 multiple choice questions related to Structured Query Language(SQL) in the first round. They will be given 15 mins to answer those questions. In round 1,the participants will be shortlisted based on their performance and eligible participants will enter into second round. The 2nd round will be conducted for 30 mins.

CRYPT YOUR MIND

Crypt your mind is a written test, where the text will be in encrypted format. Based on the key/hint the cipher text must be decrypted so to get the plain text.
