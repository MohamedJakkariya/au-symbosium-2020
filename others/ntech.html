Mine Field

GROUP ACTIVITY(3 members) One person from a team will put on a blindfold. The other person must lead their teammate from one side of the open space to the other without stepping on the objects placed on the field - using only verbal instructions. The blindfolded person cannot speak at all. If they hit an object, they must start over. The fastest team to reach other side of the field is the winner.

Mission Milliplode

The participants must stand in a line with all team members blindfolded except the last one in the line. This is no verbal instructions, The person in the back of the line will guide the participants by simply tapping the shoulders of the person infront of them,and then be guided to the bucket where they will drop the throwable. This process is continued untill all throwables have been placed in a bucket. a group can take 1-2 minutes to pre-plan. The team with least time taken to finish is the winner.

Wiz Escape

Each team is presented with a group of puzzles or clues that the teams must solve in order to find a piece of information. 1st clue is provided to each group. Based on that the mission begins with four more clues to solve to get to the finale.The team with the least time to solve the clues is the winner.
